overview

La'meer Jhon is an 18 year old artist/storyteller from Philadelphia. He was raised in California, along side his 5 sisters, mother and father. La'meer expresses himself through the art of story telling, utilizing his talents and abilities to paint pictures, and build experiences for his audience. La'meer facilitates genres such as RNB, Pop, and Country.

upcoming

La'meer started music at the age of 7. He would always move around the house humming melodies that came to his head, fiddling with instruments that laid around the house suxh as the guitar and piano, and listening heavily to music by Stevie Wonder, Chris Brown, Micheal Jackson, and a few others. One day, La'meer woke up with a whole song in his head, and made his way to his little home studio his dad set up for him to flesh it out, explaining to his dad what chords and drums sounds he wanted.

From that moment, La'meer and his dad has created a mentor/songwriting/musical development bond over the years. His dad would go on to invest heavily in La'meer, equipping him with vocal lessons, piano exercises, studio equipment and instruments. La'meer became a student, leaning along side his dad, all he knows about the art of music creation.

inspirations

Some of La'meer's biggest inspirations are Prince, Chris Brown, and Brandy. La'meer wants to be remembered as a musician as much as an artist, because of how much he appreciates the music behind the vocals, which he can look up to Prince for, while also learning a-lot from Brandy and Chris Brown when it comes to vocal arranging, stage presence and swag.

La'meer also takes big inspiration from gospel, country and rock music, using them as different flavors to add to his own sound.

No. 1 on billboards

Throughout his career, La'meer has had the opportunity to work with some incredible producers and writers, as well as artists.

One major accomplishment of his is producing a number one song (True Love) for artist J Brown, while in partnership with Theringmusicgroup.

webby award recipient

Another milestone for La'meer was becoming a webby award recipient for his work on the American Master’s Renegade Series, further solidifying him as a composer and songwriter.

stay tuned

Stay tuned to La'meer's journey by following his social media pages, and staying in the loop for up and coming music. La'meer's road wasn't an easy one, and he's come a long way, yet still only at the beginning.

La'meer already has a huge catalog of unreleased songs, from almost every genre, and is just plotting his release strategy, making sure every step he makes in the industry is impactful and meaningful to his destined legacy
